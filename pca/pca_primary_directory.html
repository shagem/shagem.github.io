Cloning products and services offer a wide variety of beneficial, repercussion free, health options. Our world class scientist are now able to offer commercial standard genetic testing and cloning procedures for eligible customers.

Gene Therapy

Gene therapy involves supplying a functional gene to cells lacking that function, with the aim of correcting a genetic disorder or acquired disease. By targeting these disfunctinal cells gene therapy aims to cure ailments which would otherwise persist through the patients life.

Cytogenetics

Cytogenetics is the study of chromosomes and chromosome abnormalities. While cytogenetics historically relied on microscopy to analyze chromosomes, new molecular technologies such as array comparative genomic hybridization are now widely available.

Predictive Medicine

Genetic testing can potentially diagnose preventable diseases, both in the individual undergoing genetic testing and in their relatives. Whole genome sequencing can establish the mutation frequency of your DNA and how to prevent it.

The Process

The PCA aims to provide the highest quality of service to our patients and customers, because of this each cloning process and service may vary and additional in-person appointments may be required.

The basic procedure begins with a simple genetic restructuring of a DNA sample provided by the patient through our at home sample kit. DNA sequencing, one of the most fundamental technologies developed to study genetics, allows researchers to determine the sequence of nucleotides in DNA fragments. Our team analyzes, repairs, and modifies the sample using computer algorithms to find and create the best possible restructuring.

After complete restructuring of the sample, the patient is then contacted with information regarding an in-person appointment time at one of our facilities. At this time the various options and procedures are discussed as well as potential future appointments.
